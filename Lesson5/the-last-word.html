The Last Word Cocktail

Supplies

  • Green Chartreuse
  • Gin of Preference
  • Luxardo Maraschino Liqueur
  • Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice
  • Luxardo Maraschino Cherry
  • Shaker Tin
  • Hawthorn Strainer
  • Fine Mesh Strainer
  • Ice Block
  • Rocks Glass

Recipe

Ingredient Amount Ratio
Gin 3/4 ounce 1
Green Chartreuse 3/4 ounce 1
Luxardo Maraschino 3/4 ounce 1
Lime Juice 3/4 ounce 1

Instructions

  1. Add large ice block to rocks glass
  2. Measure and add the ingredients to the shaker tin
  3. Add ice to the shaker tin
  4. Close shaker tin and shake vigorously for 11 seconds
  5. Open shaker tin and add the hawthorne strainer to the top of the tin
  6. With your other hand hold the fine mesh strainer over the glass
  7. Pour the contents of the shaker tin through both strainers into the glass
  8. Discard ice from shaker tin
  9. Add Luxardo Cherry to drink as garnish
